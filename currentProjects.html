Schoolyard Heroes.

What did you want to be when you were a child? And don’t give me that stock answer of “Lawyer”, “Doctor”, or even “ Programmer”. I mean deep down to your core what was it that you wanted to be. For me I wanted to be an Adventurer who slayed dragons, saved the girls, and vanquished all the evil in the land. I wanted to roam the countryside and find new adventures every day. So when recess came around, me and my friends would always gather and form our party of adventurers and scour the playground for weapons and items such as pointy sticks(swords), long sticks(lances), books(spells), throwing rocks(bow/arrow), juice pouches(health potions), and water(Ip or Imaginary Potions). With our weapons in hand we set out to find any evil doers that dare scourge our playground. That is where Schoolyard Heroes takes place. You are a kid who defeats the enemies on the battlefield(teachers, bullies, etc.), but you have to do it before recess ends.
