Design

For now there is only 5 classes.The Mage (long range spell caster),The Rogue (stealthy archer), The Knight (tanky sword and shield user), The Monk (Nimble Fist fighter), and The Priest (Support type healer). Villains will also be able to use all teh available classes as well. Every class moves and acts differently than the others. For example: The Mage class moves much slower than all the other classes but can hit very hard with long range spells while on the opposite end, the Monk class moves the fastest out of all the classes but deals the least amount of damage with his fists.
